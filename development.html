What We Do

Building a Community
Where Everyone Belongs

At FAPCAN, true transformation happens when people grow together — through shared activities, discovered talents, and the power of unity in Kidodo, Kasese.

Kidodo · Kasese
Our Community Programme

Growing Stronger,
Together

Our Community Development Programme creates a safe, active space where children, youth, and adults connect, learn, and build a stronger, united community. We believe that when people come together, they grow stronger — not just individually, but as a whole.

Through continuous engagement, we nurture talents, equip individuals with life skills, keep young people positively engaged, and foster a spirit of unity, respect, and belonging within Kidodo and beyond.

Core Activities

What We Do Together

Expression

Debates & Storytelling

We organise debates and storytelling sessions where individuals freely express themselves, find their voice, and gain hope by hearing what others have overcome and achieved.

Talent

Talent Shows & Performances

Talent shows celebrating music, dance, drama, and creative arts. These platforms help participants discover their abilities, build confidence, and inspire the wider community.

Sports

Sports & Outdoor Activities

Football, netball, and other sports for youth and elders alike — bringing joy, strengthening relationships, and creating opportunities for mentorship across generations.

Service

Community Work & Environment

Collective community tasks — digging, environmental conservation — that build a sense of shared ownership and responsibility for the neighbourhood we all call home.

How We Empower

Skills, Confidence & Unity

Nurturing Talents & Life Skills

We go beyond surface engagement. Every programme is designed to equip participants with real, lasting abilities — from public speaking and teamwork to leadership and creative expression.

  • Music, dance, and dramatic arts
  • Public speaking and confident communication
  • Teamwork and collaborative problem-solving
  • Leadership development across all age groups
  • Quiz competitions that sharpen critical thinking

Keeping Youth on a Positive Path

Positive, structured engagement keeps young people away from harmful influences. By staying active and involved, youth find purpose, direction, and community belonging.

Mentorship Across Generations

Elders, adults, and youth participate side by side — creating natural mentorship, mutual respect, and bonds of community that outlast any single event or session.

Cultural Pride & Heritage

Our storytelling and arts activities celebrate the rich cultural heritage of Kasese and Uganda — helping community members feel seen, valued, and proud of who they are.

Environmental Responsibility

Community work such as digging and conservation teaches stewardship — a sense of pride in and responsibility for the shared spaces and environment of Kidodo.

Transformation

The Impact on Lives & Community

Individuals Gain Confidence & Direction

Participants leave with a stronger sense of purpose, clearer direction in life, and the confidence to contribute positively to their families and community.

Hidden Talents Discovered

Many community members have uncovered abilities they never knew they had — in performance, leadership, and creative arts — opening doors to new opportunities.

Stronger Community Bonds

Community members actively support and uplift one another. Shared activities build genuine relationships of trust and mutual care across families and neighbourhoods.

Unity & Peace Strengthened

By bringing people together across age, background, and ability, FAPCAN's programme actively builds the peace and unity that makes Kidodo a better place for all.
