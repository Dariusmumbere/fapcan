About This Chain

From pasture to market —
quality dairy & cattle

The Dairy and Cattle Production Value Chain is one of BBUFC's core livestock chains, led by Kamuruli Livestock Farmers in partnership with Busunga Tungukye Ltd. Together they support over 100 registered cattle keepers in Kasese District.

The chain produces fresh milk, sells live cattle, and processes ghee — while Impact Integrated Agro-Enterprises Ltd and Busunga Tungukye Ltd supply feeds from agro-waste and hay to sustain herd health and productivity. Traders in fresh milk, ghee, skins and cattle form the downstream market network.

Our goal is to increase dairy productivity, reduce feed costs through agro-waste utilisation, and connect cattle keepers to fair and reliable markets for all products across the value chain.

What We Need

Required inputs
& infrastructure

The following items are needed to strengthen the dairy and cattle value chain, improve herd health and productivity, and increase the quality and volume of products reaching markets.

💧
Sustainable Safe Water Supply Reliable water supply to all pasture lands and cattle areas
🌾
Hay Storage Facilities Dry storage for hay to ensure year-round feed availability
⚙️
Shredders Crop residue shredding for agro-waste feed processing
❄️
Milk Cooling Facilities Refrigerated cooling to preserve fresh milk quality
🚐
Milk Van & Milk Containers Transport vehicle and containers for safe milk collection
🔩
Milling Machine Feed milling for processing grain and agro-waste into feeds
🏭
Pelleting Machine Pelleting equipment for compressed feed production
🌀
Fine Feeds Mixer Mixer for blending quality balanced animal feed rations
🚛
4-Ton Farm Truck Heavy-duty vehicle for feed and cattle transportation
🏗️
Factory Shed Processing shed for feed production and storage operations
🧪
Fermentation Unit Unit for fermenting agro-waste into nutritious animal feeds
🧺
Tarpaulins & Farm Tools General farm tools and tarpaulins for field operations
🧂
Fermo-Salt Mineral salt supplement to improve feed fermentation quality
🏠
Animal Houses Proper housing structures for cattle welfare and productivity
