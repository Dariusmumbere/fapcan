Save Souls
Film Industry
A FAPCAN Initiative · Kidodo, Uganda

At FAPCAN, we use film as a powerful tool for healing, expression, and transformation. Through Save Souls Film Industry, we engage young people and community members in acting, storytelling, and film production — giving them a voice to tell their own stories.

Many of the individuals we work with come from difficult backgrounds, including street life, broken homes, and trauma. Film provides a safe space where they can express emotions, rebuild confidence, and rediscover purpose.

Instead of being defined by their past, they become storytellers, actors, and creators.

Exploring Opportunities

Film Has Opened
Doors to the World

Our journey in filmmaking has created remarkable opportunities for community members who might otherwise never have left Kidodo.

Through Save Souls Film Industry, participants step beyond their limitations and explore the world while carrying their stories with them.

Travelling to different places for filming and storytelling assignments across Uganda
Connecting with wider audiences and creative networks in the film industry
Showcasing talent and culture on broader platforms including film festivals
Building partnerships with other filmmakers, NGOs, and storytelling organisations
